**Core Competencies:** Compliance architecture, cross-functional team optimization, stakeholder relationship building, and workforce transformation through strategic mentorship.

**Vision:** Thuy is driven by a dual ambition: to deepen her expertise in supply chain strategy and compliance innovation in the near term, then leverage that knowledge to shape policy and mentor the next generation of government leaders.
