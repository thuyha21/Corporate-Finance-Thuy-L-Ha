# About Ha Le Bich Thuy

Thuy holds a Bachelor of Arts in International Relations from HUFLIT and is pursuing an MBA while advancing her U.S. Army career. 

Her professional credentials include Certified Logistics Technician (CLT), Certified Logistics Associate (CLA), SOLE Demonstrated Logistician designation, Human Resource Management Certificate, and TESOL Advanced Certification—reflecting diverse expertise. 

Currently transitioning through the U.S. Army as a Unit Supply Specialist managing $1 billion+ in assets and leading 1,450+ personnel, she brings proven operational excellence and multiple Army commendations. She was recognized as the best Supply NCO of the 165th Infantry Regiment Brigade in the Army’s Command Supply Discipline Program (CSDP) competition, underscoring her ability to enforce compliance and deliver flawless accountability in high‑stakes environments.

Her foundation includes administrative leadership at De Heus LLC in Vietnam. Bilingual and culturally fluent, Ha aspires to strategic logistics consulting leveraging her military and civilian experience.

Post‑MBA, she aims to advise multinational organizations on supply chain resilience, integrating military discipline with global best practices to optimize efficiency and risk management. She is focused on expanding her expertise, building a strong professional network, and mentoring the next generation of logistics leaders.
