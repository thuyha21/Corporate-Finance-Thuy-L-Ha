## Background

Vita Coco holds ~60% of the ~$1.2B U.S. coconut-water category with FY2024 sales of $516M. It qualifies as an ideal Stage 2 case: publicly traded, audited non-financial company with complete 2019–2025 financials and testable operational thesis. Trading at a 2.6x P/E premium over peers (25.2x vs. 9.6x), the valuation is justified only if international expansion and working-capital optimization prove sustainable. Recent S&P 600 inclusion and analyst divergence (CFRA HOLD; Morningstar 3-star at $42.31 vs. $48.24) signal a time-bound opportunity. Form 10-K (2025) Items 1, 7, and 8 contain all requisite disclosures.

## Method

**Data sources:** SEC Form 10-K (2025) Items 1, 7, 8; Circana US and UK (Dec 2025); Morningstar and CFRA (April 2026).

**Techniques:** Profitability trend (revenue CAGR, margin decomposition, ROIC); working-capital decomposition (DIO, DSO, DPO, CCC); peer valuation (P/S, EV/EBITDA, forward P/E). Sensitivity analysis on margin and revenue scenarios using 10-Q/10-K schedules.

---

## Findings and Numeric Hypotheses

**Baseline:** FY2024 net margin = 10.8% (Net Income $56M / Net Sales $516M), per Form 10-K Item 8.

**Falsifiable, numeric hypotheses:**

1. **U.S. market share:** I expect Vita Coco's U.S. coconut-water market share to increase by ≥150 bps within 12 months (Circana US baseline), demonstrating pricing power and category ownership strength.

2. **International revenue growth:** I expect international revenue share to rise by 3–5 percentage points over two fiscal years (Circana UK baseline and 10-K segment data), validating geographic expansion thesis.

3. **Working-capital efficiency:** I expect the cash conversion cycle (CCC) to shorten by >10 days within 12 months via inventory optimization and improved payables/receivables terms (measured using quarterly AR, AP, and inventory days from 10-Q/10-K schedules), demonstrating operational efficiency gains.

**Validation logic:** All three hypotheses validating supports the 2.6x P/E premium; any falsification triggers re-rating pressure.
