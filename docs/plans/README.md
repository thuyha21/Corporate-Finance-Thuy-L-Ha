# Plans

Project plans, timelines, and status tracking. In professional settings, plans coordinate work across team members and communicate progress to stakeholders.

## What belongs here

- **Project timelines** — stage deadlines and milestones
- **Work plans** — task breakdowns and ownership
- **Status updates** — progress notes between stages
- **Retrospectives** — what worked, what to improve (useful after Stage 5)

**Examples:**
- `nguyen-project-timeline.md`
- `tran-stage3-workplan.md`
